Voting System Software (Phone to PC) Scenario:

- Voting System Software is initiated and loaded.
- Voter #1 casts their vote.
- Voter #1 attempts to cast another vote, but is denied.
- Voter #2 casts their vote for a different candidate.
- Voter #3 casts their vote for the SAME CANDIDATE AS VOTER #1.
- Voter #4 casts a vote that is invalid, because the candidate ID does not exist.
- The administrator of this system terminates the voting, and results are shown.
